Pricing Structure
Two-Part Billing — Industry Standard
Like all professional OOH campaigns, AutoAdz separates media costs from production costs. This is standard practice — your clients already understand this model from hoardings and transit ads.
📡 Part 1 — Campaign Fleet Fee + 🖨️ Part 2 — Creative Production
Part 1 · Media Cost
Campaign Fleet Fee
Covers everything AutoAdz operates — fleet deployment, GPS tracking, driver payouts, live dashboard, and campaign reporting.
  • Fleet deployment (20 autos)Included
  • GPS tracking — live mapIncluded
  • Driver GPS payouts (₹4.50/km)Included
  • Agency portal accessIncluded
  • QR engagement trackingIncluded
  • PDF campaign reportIncluded
  • Campaign duration (30 days)Included
Minimum fleet fee ₹1,00,000
Agency earns 15% commission on this fleet fee only. Scales with number of autos and campaign duration.
Part 2 · Production Cost
Creative Production
One-time charges for physical banner production and fitting. Billed separately, paid directly by advertiser. Standard in every OOH campaign.
  • Vinyl banner print (per auto)₹500
  • Installation & fitting (per auto)₹150
  • Removal after campaign (per auto)₹50
Total per auto ₹700
Example: 20 autos × ₹700 = ₹14,000 production charge. Billed once at campaign start. No agency commission on production charges.

Why this is better for your clients: Separating production costs means clients can compare AutoAdz fleet fees directly against other media — hoardings, bus panels, wall paintings. Our fleet fee is competitive. Production is a one-time sunk cost they already budget for in every OOH campaign. No hidden charges. No surprises at invoice time.
