Section 04
About AutoAdz

AutoAdz is a B2B2D (Business-to-Brand-to-Driver) advertising platform that transforms auto-rickshaws into GPS-tracked, data-rich mobile billboards. It is the only platform in India where brands pay for verified kilometres driven — not just ad placements.

Brands book campaigns through the AutoAdz online portal at autoadz.in, choose their target city and zone, set their budget, upload creative artwork, and instantly receive real-time GPS reports. Drivers earn per kilometre driven with the brand creative displayed on their auto.

Technology Stack
  • GPS Telematics — 10-second location updates from every active driver
  • Live Dashboard — Real-time map, km counter, zone heatmap at autoadz.in
  • Morning Photo Audit — Daily photo proof of creative display from driver app
  • QR Code Analytics — Every scan tracked with timestamp and location
  • Automated Billing — Campaign invoiced on actual GPS-verified km, not estimates

Advertising Formats

AutoAdz offers five distinct advertising formats to suit every brand objective. All formats include GPS verification, morning photo audit proofs, and daily km reports as standard.

Full Body Wrap
PREMIUM
Creative covers full auto body — sides and rear panel. Maximum visual impact and city-wide brand recall. Best for: Brand launches, product campaigns.
Rear Panel
STANDARD
Creative displayed on rear panel only. High visibility at traffic signals and crossings. Best for: Brand awareness, call-to-action campaigns.
Side Panels
STANDARD
Both left and right side panels. Ideal for route-specific zone targeting. Best for: Hyperlocal area targeting, retail campaigns.
Interior Display
ADD-ON
Small creative card inside auto for passenger engagement. Direct audience contact. Best for: QR campaigns, fintech, offers.
QR Code Integration
ADD-ON
Dynamic QR code printed on creative links to campaign landing page. Every scan tracked in real time with timestamp and location. Best for: Lead generation, digital campaigns, app downloads.

Campaign Workflow

AutoAdz delivers a seamless 10-step end-to-end campaign experience — from registration to final report — managed entirely through our technology platform.

  1. Brand registers on autoadz.in — secure multi-tenant brand portal
  2. Selects target city, zone, budget, campaign duration, and number of autos
  3. Uploads creative artwork — AutoAdz provides design templates and support
  4. AutoAdz admin reviews creative and campaign brief, approves within 24 hours
  5. Creative printed and installed on assigned auto fleet by AutoAdz team
  6. Driver morning photo check-in — proof of creative display submitted daily
  7. GPS tracking activates — every km driven with creative logged in real time
  8. Brand accesses live dashboard — impressions, km, zone heatmap, 24/7
  9. Weekly GPS reports and impression estimates emailed to brand contact
  10. Campaign ends — final performance report and GST invoice issued within 48 hours
